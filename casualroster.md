# Franchise Operations Action Matrix

This document captures quick‑win initiatives, tool options, and their trade‑offs for streamlining scheduling, payroll accuracy, change‑management, and AI demand forecasting in franchise retail operations.

---

## Legend

* **Effort** – estimated owner/manager hours to launch (1 = minimal, 5 = heavy)
* **Complexity** – technical/process sophistication required (1 = simple, 5 = complex)
* **Deployment Ease** – subjective assessment (*Easy*, *Moderate*, *Hard*)

---
